Portfolio

As a fresh face in the community I have a ton of ideas to mold, modify, and perfect into fully functional applications. I personally believe that when designing an app the usefullness of its content is right there at the top of the priorities list next to the functional consistency and the overall aesthetic. With that in mind each of my ideas will be hyper focused around optimizing the project's ability to assist and satisfy the needs and desires of any user who took the time to give my work their attention. Here are a few of my project concepts that you can look forward to seeing for yourself in the future firsthand from the comfort of your own device.

Child Chore's and More (Name in the works):
Parent assist tool to track and make child chores more interesting for them. Goal is to add in colorfull layout a reward system to entice participation. Chore list examples or a randomizer could also be exceptionally usefull.

Color Scanner Mobile:
Pretty straight forward. Uses on board camera to try to determine the color coding of anything scanned. It will be a challenge to fine tune but a pretty great tool on the fly if coded well

Pitch Perfection:
Vocal practice tool using default audio input to track pitch in time with songs. Similar to Rockband but eventually for mobile devices.

Busy Boy Chef: If you burn as much food as I have and are to lazy to look up ingredients when deciding a food plan this will help. The idea is a normal digital cook book but it will include a random meal picker function for either single day/week/month options. I myself am super lazy with cooking so this personally would help me plan and prepare food especially if it includes an "add ingredients to shopping list" button

Contractor Training Module (Training and reports modular system designed to be form fitted for different workplaces)

Lil Baby Steps (Fitness App for Normies):
This is a fitness app. Yes I know you're thinking "ugh another one?? lame," the nice thing is this one is designed with the busy body in mind. Lil Baby steps is intended to include a fitness tracker, random WoD that can be set for a single day/week/month at a time and will potentially include exercise demonstration for those of us who have had no time to keep up with workout lingo. More for getting new gym bodies into the flow of things but will be targeting hardcore gym rats as well with its workout tracker functions.

Build a Bard DnD Assistant APP:
Already have a random dice roller designed so the after cleaning up the layout of that the rest of the app will focus around pulling from archives and data bases to present statistical data of NPCs and monsters. Will also include combat tracker and character sheets creation. biggest thing that would set it aside is a built in sound/ambient noise section that can import tracks to a device for quick DM changes to the atmosphere of a scenario

More to come stay tuned!! (Name in the works)
