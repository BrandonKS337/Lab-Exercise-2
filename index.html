Portfolio

As a fresh face in the community I have a ton of ideas to mold, modify, and perfect into fully functional applications. I personally believe that when designing an app the usefullness of its content is right there at the top of the priorities list right next to the functional consistency and the overall aesthetic. With that in mind each of my ideas will be hyper focused around optimizing its ability to assist and satisfy the needs and desires of the user who took the time to give my work their attention. Here are a few of my thoughts that you can look forward to seeing for yourself in the future firsthand from your the comfort own device.

Child Chore's and More (Name in the works):
Parent assist tool to track and make child chores more interesting for them. Goal is to add in colorfull layout a reward system to entice participation. Chore list examples or a randomizer could also be exceptionally usefull.

Color Scanner Mobile

Pitch Perfection:
Vocal practice tool using default audio input to track pitch in time with songs. Similar to Rockband but eventually for mobile devices.

Busy Boy Chef

Contractor Training Module (Training and reports modular system designed to be form fitted for different workplaces)

Lil Baby Steps (Fitness App for Normies)

Build a Bard DnD Assistant APP

More to come stay tuned!! (Name in the works)
